aio_westwood_city - All in one

by klin34970 creator of aio maps
http://steamcommunity.com/id/klin34970

AIO maps contain :
- A theme (western).
- Customs players models (cowboys).
- Some funny events (jail, sheriff, hanged,...).
- One plugin for improve the map (aio_westwood_city.smx)

~ All for a total immersion ~

This plugin allow a duel when there is 1CT vs 1T.
A menu appears : Do you want duel? 
[yes] or [no].
If everyone accept you will be teleported on a duel zone with countdown and random guns.
